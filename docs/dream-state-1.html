First Dream State

I recall falling asleep once, half in a dream, half not
My cousin sat in his family van with me, we were going to the beach
I already felt sand on my feet

asleep

Brother J. holds a slingshot and asks, "like this?"
A CD flying towards my face at quickspeed

gasp

I awake with a start
