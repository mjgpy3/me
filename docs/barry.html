Gilli's Land
04 May 2022

Barry

I am not that swift little progeny; listless, flowering throughout (Claims Barry) Turn now won returns and fold your hands until lit cards disintegrate

I inch inward, inclining irises immediately – indeed – intently illumined in interest (Barry notices) Bargaining in his own brain, without my sight or knowing, Barry laughs – that maniac

Barry can barely bare the baritone bereavements of the barreling bare bear (He's had a long day) Established is he, the lad at the blessed stable, lashed lest he bled
